## Video Production Notes

### Color Scheme
- Primary: Purple (#764ba2)
- Secondary: Light purple (#667eea)
- Accent: White
- Background: Professional grays

### Font Recommendations
- Headlines: Montserrat Bold or Poppins Bold
- Body: Open Sans or Roboto
- Dhivehi text: MV Faseyha or MV Waheed

### Duration Guidelines
- Instagram Reels: 15-30 seconds
- Facebook: 30-60 seconds
- YouTube Shorts: 30-60 seconds
- Full promotional video: 90 seconds max

### Export Settings
- Resolution: 1080x1920 (vertical for stories/reels)
- Resolution: 1920x1080 (horizontal for YouTube/Facebook)
- Frame rate: 30fps
- Format: MP4 (H.264)
